TransferWatch

Airport transfer management for professional drivers. Know the moment a flight changes — before you leave for the airport.

Features

Everything a transfer driver needs

✈️

Automatic Flight Detection

Add a flight number anywhere in your calendar event — title, notes, or location — and TransferWatch finds it automatically.

🛬

Real-Time Arrival Tracking

Powered by AeroDataBox. See live status, estimated arrival time, and delay information for every booking.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Alerts for delays over 30 minutes, early arrivals, cancellations, and automatic reminders 1 hour before pickup.

🚗

Departure Bookings

Calendar events without flight numbers appear as departure cards — dropoffs are tracked alongside arrivals in one timeline.

📋

Client Details On-Card

Tap any card to reveal the client's phone number, pickup address, and booking notes. One tap to call, text, or open Maps.

⚡️

Instant Calendar Sync

Add or remove a booking and it appears or disappears in the app immediately — no manual refresh needed.

📅

Browse Future Days

Swipe left to step forward day by day and see what's booked ahead. Swipe right to return to today. No API calls — calendar only.

📱

Home & Lock Screen Widgets

Glance at your next pickup without unlocking. Medium widget shows flight, passenger, ETA, and delay. Lock screen complications for circular, rectangular, and inline styles.

🏝️

Dynamic Island & Live Activities

While a flight is in the air, the Dynamic Island and lock screen show live status, arrival time, and delays — always one glance away.

Timeline

Notifications

Always in the loop

🛬

1 Hour Reminder

Automatic alert one hour before every tracked arrival so you're never caught out.

🚗

Departure Reminder

30-minute heads-up before every airport dropoff booking.

⚠️

Delay Alerts

Notified when a flight is delayed more than 30 minutes, and again as the delay grows.

✅

Early Arrivals

Know immediately when a flight lands ahead of schedule.

🚨

Cancellations

Instant alert if a tracked flight is cancelled so you can contact your client right away.

⏰

Booking Mismatch

Flagged when the scheduled arrival doesn't match your calendar booking time.
