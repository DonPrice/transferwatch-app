Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

✅ The short version

TransferWatch does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything stays on your device.

1. Overview

TransferWatch ("the app") is developed by Donovan Price for use by airport transfer drivers. This privacy policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

TransferWatch is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app does not operate any servers, does not create user accounts, and does not transmit personal information to any party other than the flight data API described below.

2. Calendar Access

TransferWatch requests full access to your device's calendar in order to:

  • Read event titles, notes, and locations to detect flight numbers
  • Display booking details (client name, address, phone) within the app
  • Monitor changes to calendar events in real time

Your calendar data is never transmitted outside your device. It is read locally and used only to display information within the app. No calendar content is sent to any server operated by the app developer.

3. Flight Data — AeroDataBox

To look up live flight status, TransferWatch sends flight numbers (e.g. QF404) to AeroDataBox, a third-party aviation data service accessed via the API.market gateway. The only information sent is the flight identifier and a date window.

No personal information is included in these requests. Client names, phone numbers, addresses, and calendar notes are never sent to AeroDataBox, API.market, or any other service.

API.market's privacy policy is available at api.market/privacy_policy.

The AeroDataBox API key you enter in Settings is stored only in your device's local storage (UserDefaults) and is never transmitted to any party other than the AeroDataBox API endpoint.

4. Notifications

TransferWatch uses Apple's local notification system to deliver alerts about flight status changes, reminders, and delays. All notifications are generated on your device. No notification content is sent to any external server.

5. Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults and standard app storage:

  • Your selected calendar identifier
  • Your AeroDataBox API key
  • Cached flight status data (to persist between app launches)

This data is stored only on your device and is removed when you delete the app. It is not backed up to iCloud or any other cloud service.

6. Third-Party Services

TransferWatch uses one third-party service:

  • AeroDataBox (via API.market) — receives flight identifiers only (no personal data). See their privacy policy at api.market/privacy_policy.

The app does not use analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or any other third-party SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

TransferWatch is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
